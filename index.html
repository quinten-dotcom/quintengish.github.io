Questions you are probably asking.

I have hired ad people before and got burned.

Most media buyers do not understand high ticket funnels. They optimize for clicks, not booked calls or closed deals. I handle strategy, copy, scripts, and ads. The full picture, not just the ad manager.

I am not sure paid ads work for coaching.

I have generated 30x ROAS for a coaching launch and $144K+ for a consulting firm. Paid media works when the funnel, creative, and targeting are built for high ticket. That is what I do.

What if I do not have a massive budget?

High ticket means you do not need volume. You need the right people seeing the right message. Some of my best results came from lean budgets with sharp targeting and strong creative.
